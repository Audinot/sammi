Sammi Lee - Biography

Sammi was born into a family of artists, discovering the joy of piano at the age of five and loving any form of the arts since. As a pianist, classical solo singer, dancer, actress, choir member, and rock band artist, she is a multi-talented performer who brings her work to all sorts of stages. Sammi has a passion for sharing music, not only through her performances but also through teaching and encouraging students.

Sammi Lee has acquired her MMT Master of Music Therapy, and also her BFA Bachelor of Fine Arts with Honours (with a specialization in Classical Piano) at York University. She has also been trained in guitar, vocal, and Orff instruments.

Sammi has experience teaching music, in private schools and through in-home lessons worldwide. She utilizes repertoire of all types of music literature, including traditional and contemporary, that is appropriate for all ages and skill levels to provide creative and professional music lessons. Sammi's teaching methods are adaptive to the varying aptitudes of each student. She appreciates instilling the joy of learning and making music in each of her students.
